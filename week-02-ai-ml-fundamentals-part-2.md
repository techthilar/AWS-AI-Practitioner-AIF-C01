- Data types: labeled/unlabeled, tabular, time-series, image, text, structured/unstructured
- Learning types: supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement

- Techniques per use case: regression, classification, clustering
- AWS managed AI services: SageMaker AI, Transcribe, Translate, Comprehend, Lex, Polly
- AI/ML pipeline stages, MLOps concepts, model metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, F1) vs. business metrics (ROI, cost per user)
